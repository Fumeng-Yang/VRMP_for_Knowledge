Experiment Overview

There are two main tasks in this experiment:

1) Read and remember a series of passages from scientific publications.
2) Recall the passages you read as complete as you can.

The following sessions are: explanation of our expectation - practice - main session.

There might be latin names in the passages. You can straightly ignore them.
Remember to turn off or stay away from your cell phone(s) and other electronic devices that can interrupt you.
