Experiment Overview

There are three main tasks in this experiment:

1) Learn a memory method called Memory Palace (also called Method of loci).
2) We will give you a set of locations, and you will use them as your palace to read and remember a series of passages from scientific publications.
3) Recall the passages as complete as you can.

The following sessions are: explanation of our expectation - training - practice - main session.

There might be latin names in the passages. You can straightly ignore them.
Remember to turn off or stay away from your cell phone(s) and other electronic devices that can interrupt you.
