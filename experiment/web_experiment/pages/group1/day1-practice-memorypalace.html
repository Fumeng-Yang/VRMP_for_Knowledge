Part 0 - Practice Session

The desk and the table will be used as your palace to build the connection (the imaginary visualization).

The tasks for you are:

1) carefully read the passage on the next page. We blur the reading material on purposes. You need to move the mouse onto the passage in order to read it. Try the technique here: (test).

How to read the passages:
Read the passage at your own pace and make sure each sentence makes sense to you. You will be asked to recall as much of the passage as you could remember afterwards. But please do not attempt to memorize the passage word by word.

2) build a connection between the passage and the desk using your imagination and the memory palace method.

How to build a connection:
Try to visualize what you read as objects, stories, images, and animations. The visualization should be as incredible and appealing as possible. It should provide you enough hints to help you recall the content of the passage later. Repeat the imagination multiple times and confirm yourself you are not going to change the visualization.
